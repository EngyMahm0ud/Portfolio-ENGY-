Overview

ElAjans is a dedicated section of the Sylndr platform for buying and selling used cars in Egypt in a reliable and hassle-free way. The website offers a streamlined digital experience with thoroughly inspected cars and multiple financing options. ElAjans aims to provide a safe and professional alternative to traditional car dealerships through an online journey.

We had an intial request

Based on analytics and customer support feedback, the adoption rate for "Buy via SYLNDR" is significantly lower across most listings which impacts the business in several ways : Lost Revenue - Increased Support Costs - Brand Experience Risk.

Strategy Questions

BUSINESS GOALS

  1. Increase the revenue
  2. Decrease support costs
  3. Opportunity to expand

PRODUCT OBJECTIVES

  1. People send requests successfully and they understand what they send.
  2. Increase the number of users who complete the "Buy via SYLNDR" journey.

DESIGN OBJECTIVES

  1. Discover What make people confused about "Buy via SYLNDR" after complete the flow.
  2. Find solutions for the problems we will find
  3. Test the solution with users to measure design impact and effectiveness.
